Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Hitsona different from big-box gyms?

Hitsona offers a non-intimidating, community-driven environment with guided workouts designed for real results. No machines, no mirrors—just expert coaching and support to help you succeed.

Do I need to be in shape to start?

Not at all! Our workouts are designed for all fitness levels, and our coaches will customize exercises to match your abilities and help you progress at your own pace.

What types of workouts do you offer?

We offer group-based personal training sessions focused on strength, fat loss, endurance, and overall health. Our workouts combine functional movements, resistance training, and cardio for maximum results.

Is nutrition support included?

Absolutely! All memberships include unlimited nutritional guidance and accountability coaching.
